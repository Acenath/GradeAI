Quick Tutorial

Teacher Guide

Creating Section

Teachers can create course sections and add students manually or by uploading a CSV file. To create a section:

  1. Navigate to your dashboard
  2. Click "Add Section" button
  3. Enter course details or upload a CSV with student information

Rubric Creation Management

Create customized rubrics for evaluating student work:

  1. Go to "Assignments" section
  2. Select "Create Assignment"
  3. Define rubric criteria, point values, and descriptions
  4. Save the rubric for future use

Student Guide

Submit Essay

Students can submit essays in a few simple steps:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Select the appropriate course and assignment
  3. Upload your essay (supported formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  4. Click "Submit" to complete the process

View Feedback

Once your submission has been graded:

  1. Navigate to the assignment in your dashboard
  2. Click on the assignment to see your grade and detailed feedback
  3. Review highlighted strengths and areas for improvement

General Tips

  • Regular browser updates ensure the best platform experience
  • Set email notifications to stay informed about deadlines and feedback
  • Use the "Contact Support" feature if you encounter any issues
